Types of photography

City/Street
Street photography is my favourite! I love the mix of old and new architecture, traffic, people... I'm a city person and I love how full of life cities are.
Portraits
I love portrait photography, trying to capture the spirit of someone in an image is a constant challenge. I will never tire of photographing people, for this very reason.
Animals/Nature
I'm a huge animal lover. Aside from trying to get them to stay still, it's also a challenge to capture their individual personality.
Time Lapse
Another of my favourite types of photography. The biggest challenge is getting the right composition, with enough mix of static and moving objects. I love it.
